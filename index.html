How to play Oware

Number of players: 2

Display: Oware board is setup to be 2 rows of 6 holes which should be referred to as houses.

  • Each House needs to have 4 marbles when setting up the game

    • Objective: NKONIM! With the player who gets the most marbles

    instructions:

    • *Players must go one at a time**Move counter-clockwise*
    • Each Hole represent a House
    • Players must click on a House within their side to start the game. As that happens the mables will be distributed around the broad
    • When a House is filled with at least 12 marbles , that current player will receive that house
    • The player with the most marbles has a NKONIM(Victory)!
